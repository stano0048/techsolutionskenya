Pricing FAQs

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers (local and international), PayPal, and cryptocurrency (BTC, USDT). Payments can be made in USD or KES equivalent.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We typically work on a 50/50 payment structure (50% upfront, 50% on delivery). For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

What's included in the support period?

Our support includes bug fixes, minor adjustments, and troubleshooting. Major feature additions would be quoted separately. Response times vary by plan.
