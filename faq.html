Frequently Asked Questions

Here's everything you need to know before working with us. Can't find your question? Contact us directly — we're happy to help!

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity.

  • Basic Website: 1-2 weeks
  • Custom Web App: 2-4 weeks
  • Mobile App: 3-6 weeks
  • Trading Bots or AI Tools: 2-5 weeks

We'll give you a personalized timeline during your consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment options to ensure convenience:

  • M-Pesa (Till or Paybill)
  • Bank Transfer (Local or International)
  • PayPal
  • Crypto (BTC, USDT, etc.)

We usually work on a 50/50 model: 50% upfront and 50% upon delivery. For larger projects, we can discuss milestone-based payments.

How do I get started with a project?

Getting started is simple:

  1. Reach Out: Use the contact form or WhatsApp us.
  2. Consultation: We'll schedule a call to understand your needs.
  3. Proposal & Quote: We send a detailed proposal with pricing and timelines.
  4. Onboarding: Once we agree, we begin your project and keep you in the loop.

What's your revisions policy?

We include up to 3 rounds of free revisions in every project to ensure your satisfaction.

Additional revisions beyond this may incur a small fee, depending on the scope of change.

We always aim for perfection the first time — but we're flexible and open to tweaks.

Do you offer maintenance after project completion?

Yes, we offer three maintenance packages:

  • Basic: Security updates and bug fixes
  • Standard: Basic + performance optimization
  • Premium: Standard + content updates and 24/7 support

All new projects come with 1 month free basic maintenance.

What technologies do you specialize in?

Our core technologies include:

  • Web: React, Node.js, Python (Django/Flask)
  • Mobile: Flutter, React Native
  • AI/ML: TensorFlow, PyTorch
  • Blockchain: Solidity, Web3.js
  • Automation: Python, Selenium, Zapier

We're here to help you with any questions about our services or process.
